## What counts as a translation?

Each call to [`/v1/medical/translate`](/api-reference/translate/) or [`/v1/medical/safe-translate`](/api-reference/safe-translate/) counts as **one translation**, regardless of text length.

---

## Overages

You'll receive a warning at **80% usage**. Overage is billed at the per-translation rate for your tier. Tolka never cuts off access mid-request.

---

## Switching plans

You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

---

## Platform partners

Building a telehealth or EHR platform? A **revenue-share model** is available for deep integrations. Contact [sales@tolka.health](mailto:sales@tolka.health).

---

## Pilot programme

We offer a **3-month pilot** with Pro-tier access at no cost. Community health organizations can contact us for discounted Starter pricing. Apply at [tolka.health/pilot](https://docs.tolka.health/signup).
